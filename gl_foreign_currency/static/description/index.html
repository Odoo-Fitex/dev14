User Guide

After installing the app, navigate to 'General Ledger' under 'Reports' from 'Accounting'

You can currently view and export the report and all of the numbers in your company's default currency but you can navigate to 'Currency' & choose one of the active currencies in which you would like to generate the report.

Voila! Notice how the numbers and the currency change.

You can also export the report in 'xslx' form displaying your numbers depending on the currency you have chosen to view the report.
